clause_text:  and hereinafter referred to as the

civil_code: Article 1651, Article 1169

clause_function: Term of Lease
clause_text: That the lease shall be for a period of one year from December 17, 2002 up to December 17,2003;
civil_code: Article 1654, Article 1657

clause_function: Rental Amount
clause_text: That the LESSEE shall pay a monthly rental of FIFTY THOUSAND PESOS (P50,000.00), Philippine Currency, payable every 16th day of the month;
civil_code: Article 1649

clause_function: Security Deposit and Advance Payment
clause_text: That the LESSEE upon ________________________________________________ PESOS
(_______________) representing two (2) months deposit and one (1) month advance. That the
deposit shall not be considered as payment for rental but will be reimbursed by the LESSOR
upon the termination of this contract, unless the leased property suffers damages or the LESSEE has unpaid utility and water bills in which case, said bills and damages will be paid out of the deposit
civil_code: Article 1651, Article 1169

clause_function: Utilities Responsibility
clause_text:
Electric and water bills shall be for the account of the LESSEE, based on the monthly meter readings.
civil_code: Article 1654, Article 1657

clause_fcuntion: Use of Premises
clause_text: The leased premises shall be devoted exclusively for residential purposes only.
civil_code: Article 1657

clause_function: Improvements and Alterations
clause_text: All improvements require prior consent of the LESSOR, and any improvement that cannot be removed shall become the property of the LESSOR.
civil_codE: Article 1678, Article 1661

clause_function: Repairs and Damages
clause_text: Expenses for repairs arising from willful and knowing damage caused by the LESSEE shall be shouldered by the LESSEE
civil_code: Article 1654, Article 1657

clause_function: Subleasing Prohibition
clause_text: The leased premises shall not be subleased to any person or entity without the written consent of the LESSOR.
civil_code: Article 1650

clause_function: Failure to Surrender Premises
clause_text: If the premises are not surrendered upon expiration of the lease, the LESSEE shall be liable for all damages suffered by the LESSOR.
civil_code: Article 1673, Article 1167

clause_function: Condition Upon Return
clause_text: The LESSEE agrees to return the premises in good condition, subject only to reasonable wear and tear, without delay
civil_code: Article 1665

clause_function: Lessor’s Right to Offset Arrears
clause_text: In case of unpaid rental arrears, the LESSOR may take possession of the LESSEE’s tangible and valuable property to offset the arrears without judicial action.
civil_code: Article 1662, Article 2241

clause_function: Abandonment of Premises
clause_text: If the premises are abandoned for one (1) week without notice and while the LESSEE is in default, the LESSOR may consider the premises legally abandoned and take possession thereof.
civil_code: Article 1673, Article 1658

clause_function: Rental Escalation
clause_text:The LESSOR reserves the right to increase the rental rate by ten percent (10%) after one (1) year if the contract is renewed by mutual agreement. 
civil_code: Article 1306, Article 1643

clause_function: Cleanliness and Conduct
clause_text: The LESSEE is obliged to observe cleanliness and maintain a peaceful condition inside and outside the premises.
civil_code: Article 1657, Article 1663

clause_function: Compliance and Sanctions
clause_text: The LESSEE warrants having read and understood the contract and agrees to comply; violations may result in ejectment and other legal sanctions.
civil_code: Article 1159, Article 1673
